How to Use

  1. Fill in your information in the form.
  2. Preview your signature on the right.
  3. Click "Copy Signature to Clipboard"
  4. Open Gmail, click on Settings (the cog on the upper right).
  5. Select "See all settings" at the top of the menu.
  6. Scroll down the page to find the Signature section of the Settings.
  7. Delete your existing signature and press paste (Windows: CTRL + V; Mac: CMD + V) to add your updated signature.
